A workspace is your primary environment in Oculon where teams collaborate on models, boards, and databases. You can have multiple workspaces for different teams, projects, or use cases.

![](/images/getting-started/workspace-list.png)

## Switching & Managing Workspaces

You can access all your workspaces from the profile menu:

- Click on your **profile icon (top-left)**
- Select **Switch Workspace**
- Choose from the list of available workspaces

From here, you can also:

- **Create a new workspace**
- **Join an existing workspace**

This makes it easy to separate work across teams (e.g., Finance, Growth, Ops).


## Workspace Types

Workspaces can be configured based on how users join:

- **Public**  
  Anyone can request or join the workspace without an invite

- **Private**  
  Only users invited by an admin can join


## Workspace Settings

Workspace admins can manage settings from:

**Settings → General**

![](/images/getting-started/general-settings.png)

### What you can manage

- **Workspace Name**  
  Update the name of your workspace

- **Access Type (Public / Private)**  
  Control how users join the workspace

- **Delete Workspace**  
  Permanently delete the workspace and all its content

## Best Practices

- Use separate workspaces for **different teams or environments**  
- Keep sensitive workspaces **private**  
- Use public workspaces for **open collaboration or demos**
