Manage who has access to your workspace, what they can do, and how they are grouped. This helps teams collaborate securely while maintaining control over permissions.

![](/images/getting-started/users.png)

## User States

Users in your workspace can be in different states based on how they joined:

- **Requested**  
  Users who request access to a public workspace.  
  You can **accept or reject** these requests.

- **Invited**  
  Users invited via email.  
  You can **revoke the invite** if needed before they join.

- **Active**  
  Users who are part of the workspace and can access resources based on their role.

---

## Inviting Users

You can invite users directly to your workspace:

- Click **Invite People**
- Enter **one or multiple email IDs**
- Assign roles and groups (optional)
- Send the invite

---

## Roles

Roles define what a user can do in the workspace:

- **Member**  
  Can view and work on models, boards, and databases based on access permissions.

- **Workspace Owner**  
  Full control over workspace settings, users, and permissions.

---

## Groups

Groups help organize users and manage access more efficiently.

- Add users to **one or multiple groups**
- Assign permissions at the group level
- Useful for teams (e.g., Finance, Sales, Ops)

---

## Key Actions

- Approve or reject access requests  
- Invite or revoke users  
- Assign roles (Member / Workspace Owner)  
- Add users to groups for structured access
