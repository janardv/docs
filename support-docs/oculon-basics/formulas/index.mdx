## **Formula:**

With Formula you can work with variable, numbers, time and ranges. You can preview your formula in the top bar. **Formula Column**: You can hide and unhide by toggle on in the toolbar.

## **Inserting a formula:**

The syntax and working method of formula is same as Spreadsheet or Excel. Making it easy for you to set up calculations quickly. If you're familiar with Excel functions, you'll feel right at home!

**How to Insert a Formula?**

1. Click on the Formula cell: Select the formula cell where you want to apply a formula.
2. Type Your Formula: Use your predefined variables to define your logic.

Example: =Revenue - Cost for calculating profit

1. Press Enter: Watch your calculation appear instantly!

## **How can I use variable in the formula cell?**

Just type its name, and an autocomplete dropdown will pop up—select it from there. If the variable is from another model and it’s linked, you can reference that too.
