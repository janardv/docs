# Boards

**What Are Charts?**

Charts help you visualize your financial models clearly and intuitively. Instead of analyzing raw numbers, you can use charts to spot trends, compare scenarios and versions, and make data-driven decisions faster.

Whether you need a simple line graph for revenue growth or a complex waterfall chart for cash flow, Oculon’s charting feature has covered you!

**How to Create a Chart**

1️⃣ Click "Dashboard" – Find the chart option at the top bar on the dashboard page.

2️⃣ Pick a Chart Type – Line, Bar, Pie, Waterfall, Area… whatever works best!

3️⃣ Select Model – Choose the Model from your model list.

4️⃣ Select Data – Choose the variables, scenarios, and time range you want to visualize.

5️⃣ Customize It – Adjust colors, labels, and legends for clarity.

6️⃣ Save & Share – Embed the chart in your dashboard or export it as an image/PDF.

## **Working with Charts**

Update Data – Your charts update automatically when your model changes!

Customize Appearance – Modify axes, legends, colors, and annotations.

Link to Dashboards – Combine multiple charts into a dashboard for a holistic view.

Export & Share – Download as images, PDFs, or share interactive links.

### **Why Use Charts?**

Quickly Understand Data – Spot trends, patterns, and anomalies at a glance.

Simplify complex things - Scenarios, Versions, Goals, Benchmarks: See the impact of different business assumptions.

Present Insights Effectively – Communicate key takeaways with stakeholders.

Save Time – No need to manually crunch numbers.

### Create Dashboards

Dashboards are the perfect way to present your model, metrics, KPIs in a clean, interactive, and visually appealing format. They allow others to view key insights, explore input variables, and interact with your data.

You can combine multiple chart type, tables, KPI cards and texts makes a beautiful and well organised dashboard.

From the tob bar, select the type of chart and insert it on the dashboard. Later configure the charts based on your required view and needs.
