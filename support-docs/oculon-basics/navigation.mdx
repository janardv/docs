**Top Utility Bar**

The Top Utility Bar is your go-to space for quick access to Oculon AI’s key functions:

**Breadcrumb Navigation**: Located below the utility bar, Breadcrumbs display your current location within the app and help you backtrack to previous pages or workspaces.

- **Tip**: Use the breadcrumbs to navigate back instead of using your browser’s back button, as this preserves your position within Oculon AI.

**Search**: Easily locate people, groups, reports, and scenarios across Oculon AI.

- **How to Use**: Click in the search bar or press (Ctrl/ Command + K), type a keyword (like “Quarterly Sales Report”), and see categorized results, allowing you to quickly find what you’re looking for.

**Help**: Access resources and support.

- **Options**:
    - **Documentation**: Search our help centre for articles and FAQs.
    - **Tutorials**: Watch video guides to get up to speed on platform features.
    - **Oculon AI**: Use Oculon’s AI assistant for instant answers to questions.
    - **Contact Support**: Reach out to our support team for any specific needs.

**Notifications**: Displays alerts for updates to your account, such as new comments, data changes, or updates on models you’re following.

**Sensitive Data Toggle**: A helpful privacy tool, this toggle allows you to hide or show sensitive data, such as financial figures. It’s especially useful for screen-sharing sessions or in public settings.

**Profile Menu**: This includes your profile and account settings, allowing you to manage personal details.

**Options**:

- **My Profile**: View and edit your profile information.
- **User Settings**: Update your profile picture, change your password, and adjust notification preferences.
- **Sign Out**: Log out of your Oculon AI account.

**Workspace**: This displays the Workspace you’re currently working within. If you have access to multiple Workspace, click the Workspace name to switch between accounts.

### **Left Sidebar Navigation**

The Left Sidebar provides access to Oculon AI’s main sections, enabling you to explore and manage models, dashboards, templates, and more. Your sidebar options depend on your **role and permissions** within Oculon AI and the **modules** your organization subscribes to.

**Sidebar Items:**

1. **Collapse Menu**: Click this button at the top of the sidebar to show or hide menu item names, making it easier to manage your screen space.
2. **Profile**: Access your profile directly, allowing you to complete forms, view role-based information, or make updates as needed.
3. **Model Studio**: The central workspace for creating, exploring, and analysing your models.
    - **How to Use**: Click **Model Studio** to view a list of models you have access to or create a new one. You can open a specific model, review key data, or make edits.
4. **Dashboards**: View and manage data visualizations and charts.
    - **How to Use**: Dashboards allow you to track and analyse metrics across models. Click **Dashboards** to view existing charts or create new ones, giving you an overview of performance metrics and key insights.
5. **Templates**: Access pre-built templates for a variety of financial models, designed to help you get started quickly. Use templates to jump-start model creation by choosing ones that closely match your needs. You can modify any template to fit your organization’s data.
6. **Library**: Access a comprehensive library of metrics and formulas. In Library, you can browse and select commonly used metrics or formulas, such as KPIs and financial equations, to apply directly within your models.
7. **Database:**

The Database feature in Oculon lets you organize and manage large datasets efficiently.

Database is itself a amazing feature, it enables you to link, view, share and collaborate to your financial models. Think of it as a centralized place where all your raw data resides, ready to be pulled into your models when needed.

1. **Favourite:**

You can mark your charts, models, templates, database as favourite and access it from the favourite icon from the left panel. It helps you to access quickly wih the most used, most liked items on Ocluon.

10. **Teamspace**:

A **Teamspace** in Oculon is your dedicated workspace for organizing models, dashboards, documents and data with a specific team or department. It helps structure your work better, collaborate efficiently, and manage access control.
