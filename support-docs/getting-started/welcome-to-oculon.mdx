# **Welcome to Oculon! 🚀**

Oculon is your **next-gen financial planning and analysis tool**, designed to make complex financial modeling a breeze. Whether you’re forecasting revenue, analyzing multiple scenarios, or collaborating with your team in real-time, Oculon gives you everything you need in one powerful platform.

Here’s what you can do with Oculon:

### **Model Studio**

Build and manage **dynamic financial models** with ease. Create multiple scenarios, analyze outcomes, and stay ahead of business changes.

### **Dashboards**

Visualize key metrics and performance indicators. **Customizable and interactive dashboards** help you turn data into actionable insights.

### **Database**

Centralize and manage your data. **Connect external sources** and use real-time information in your models.

### Library

From Library you can access your collection of Integrations, Databases, Metrics and Dimensions.

### **Access & Security**

Collaborate securely with **custom permissions**. Share models, dashboards, and databases with the right level of access.

## **Getting Started**

**Set Up Your First Model** – Head to the **Model Studio** and start creating.

**Connect Your Data** – Use the Database feature to import or link your data.
