**Top Utility Bar**

The Top Utility Bar is your go-to space for quick access to Oculon AI’s key functions:

**Breadcrumb Navigation**: Located below the utility bar, Breadcrumbs display your current location within the app and help you backtrack to previous pages or workspaces.

- **Tip**: Use the breadcrumbs to navigate back instead of using your browser’s back button, as this preserves your position within Oculon AI.

![](/images/getting-started/navigation.png)

**Profile Menu**: This includes your profile and account settings, allowing you to manage personal details.

![](/images/getting-started/nav-options.png)

**Options**:

- **My Profile**: View and edit your profile information.
- **User Settings**: Update your profile picture, change your password, and adjust notification preferences.
- **Sign Out**: Log out of your Oculon AI account.

**Workspace**: This displays the Workspace you’re currently working within. If you have access to multiple Workspace, click the Workspace name to switch between accounts.

### **Left Sidebar Navigation**

The Left Sidebar provides access to Oculon AI’s main sections, enabling you to explore and manage models, dashboards, templates, and more. Your sidebar options depend on your **role and permissions** within Oculon AI and the **modules** your organization subscribes to.

**Sidebar Items:**

1. **Collapse Menu**: Click this button at the top of the sidebar to show or hide menu item names, making it easier to manage your screen space.
2. **Profile**: Access your profile directly, allowing you to complete forms, view role-based information, or make updates as needed.
3. **Model Studio**: The central workspace for creating, exploring, and analysing your models.
    - **How to Use**: Click **Model Studio** to view a list of models you have access to or create a new one. You can open a specific model, review key data, or make edits.
5. **Dashboards**: View and manage data visualizations and charts.
    - **How to Use**: Dashboards allow you to track and analyse metrics across models. Click **Dashboards** to view existing charts or create new ones, giving you an overview of performance metrics and key insights.
6. **Templates**: Access pre-built templates for a variety of financial models, designed to help you get started quickly.
    - **Tip**: Use templates to jump-start model creation by choosing ones that closely match your needs. You can modify any template to fit your organization’s data.
7. **Library**: Access a comprehensive library of metrics and formulas.
    - **How to Use**: In **Libraries**, you can browse and select commonly used metrics or formulas, such as KPIs and financial equations, to apply directly within your models.
8. **Database:** Create, View and perform other actions with database to analyse the data you want in the way you want.
    1. You can create database from running a query after integrating your tool.
    2. You can share your Database with your team members.
    3. You can edit and manipulate database with according to your needs.
