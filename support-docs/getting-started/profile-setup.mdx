### **Profile Setup**

After logging in, set up your profile for a personalized experience and to make it easy for teammates to identify you.

![](/images/getting-started/settings-profile.png)

**Updating Personal Information**

1. **Go to Profile Settings**: Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner, then select **Settings** > **Profile**.
2. **Add Profile Details**:
    - **Name**: Enter your full name.
    - **Email**: Enter your work email.
    - **Phone Number:** Enter your phone number.
    - **Password**: Set Your Password
3. **Save Changes**: Click **Save** to confirm your updates.

**Configuring Preferences**

![](/images/getting-started/preferences.png)

**Display Preference:**

Theme:

- System default
- Light mode
- Dark mode

The Theme selector lets you switch between system default, light, and dark themes to control how the interface is displayed.
