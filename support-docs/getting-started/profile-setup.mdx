### **Profile Setup**

After logging in, set up your profile for a personalized experience and to make it easy for teammates to identify you.

![](/images/getting-started/settings-profile.png)

**Updating Personal Information**

1. **Go to Profile Settings**: Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner, then select **Settings** > **Profile**.
2. **Add Profile Details**:
    - **Name**: Enter your full name.
    - **Email**: Enter your work email.
    - **Phone Number:** Enter your phone number.
    - **Password**: Set Your Password
3. **Upload Profile Picture** (optional): Click **Upload** to add a profile picture.
4. **Save Changes**: Click **Save** to confirm your updates.

### **Notification Settings:**

**Login Notification:**

Receive alerts whenever your account is accessed from a new device or location.

**Update Alert:**

Stay informed about critical updates to your models, scenarios, or shared workspaces.

Promotional and New Feature Alerts:

Get notified about upcoming promotions, new features, or special offers from Oculon.

**Timezone:**

Set your preferred timezone to ensure accurate timestamps for reports, notifications, and scheduled tasks.

**Display Preference:**

Predefined Text Sizes:

- Small
- Medium (Default)
- Large

Larger text size for presentations or detailed reviews. Smaller text size to fit more information on the screen.

**Delete Account:**

For users who wish to discontinue using Oculon and remove all traces of their account.
