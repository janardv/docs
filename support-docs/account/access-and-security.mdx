Oculon’s **Access & Security** features are designed to ensure that your data and models remain secure while allowing seamless collaboration across teams. These settings empower you to control who can access your work, what they can do, and how data security is enforced.

### **Key Features of Access & Security**

**User Roles & Permissions**

Assign **Viewer**, **Editor**, or **Owner** roles to manage access levels for models, dashboards, and databases.

Granular permissions allow specific control at the section, variable, or dataset level.

**Secure Sharing Options**

Share via secure links with expiration dates or restricted access.

Control external sharing to ensure sensitive data is not exposed.

**Authentication & Login**

Password policies to enforce strong authentication practices.

Google Sign-In: Convenient and secure one-click login with your Google account.

OTP Verification: Get a one-time passcode sent to your email and verify while login in.

**Data Encryption**

End-to-end encryption for data at rest and in transit.

Secure APIs for integrations.

**Activity Monitoring**

Detailed audit trails to track access, changes, and activity history.

Notifications for unauthorized or unusual activities.
