**Login Notification:**

Receive alerts whenever your account is accessed from a new device or location.

**Update Alert:**

Stay informed about critical updates to your models, scenarios, or shared workspaces.

**Promotional and New Feature Alerts:**

Get notified about upcoming promotions, new features, or special offers from Oculon.

### **How to Manage Notification Settings**

Follow these simple steps to customize your notification preferences in Oculon:

**Step 1: Access Notification Settings**

1. Open the **Settings** section of the app or website.
2. From the side panel, select **Preferences**.

**Step 2: Customize Notifications**

- **Login Notifications**: Toggle on/off to enable or disable alerts for security-related activities such as login attempts or suspicious access.
- **Update Alerts**: Choose the level of updates you want to receive:
    - All updates across models and dashboards.
    - Specific models or dashboards of your choice.
- **Promotional Alerts**: Opt-in or out of receiving marketing emails, feature announcements, and special offers.

**Step 3: Save Your Preferences**

- Once you’ve customized your settings, click **Save** to ensure your preferences are applied.

You can revisit these settings anytime to make adjustments based on your preferences or workload. For more details, check the **Help Center** or reach out to **support@oculon.ai**.
